Service Center(Senla)

Service Center (https://mastera.info)

Online organization of repair service center

This is final project of Menko Mikhail on Senla training.

There is rights separation.

At the moment there are four user types (Customer, Engineer, Receiver, Admin)

They all will have different operation option to use in program. And of course Admin is Master.

Used technologies:

  • JSF 2
  • JSR 330
  • JUnit 4
  • JWT (JJWT)
  • Hibernate 5
  • Log4j
  • MySQL
  • Primefaces
  • Tomcat 9
  • Spring MVC
